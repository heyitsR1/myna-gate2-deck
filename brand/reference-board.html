Sangam · The Trust Bet

Trust references

Institutional, restrained, navy-and-brass — quiet authority through material and structure, not decoration.

Bloomberg Terminal

One accent color, dark ground, 40 years running.

BorrowONE accent (brass) on dark, never a second hue — same discipline for dashboard status states.
JPMorganChase

2024 rebrand added one warm neutral to cold institutional blue.

BorrowValidates Sangam's 60/30/10 navy+brass ratio — a bank-tested move, not a guess.
Mercury

Trust through restraint — noise hidden by default.

BorrowCollapse channel badges/timestamps into a calm thread by default; expand on click.
Maina · The Human Bet

Human references

Warm, candid, documentary — real people over stock people; the photography carries the brand more than the type does.

Airbnb, 2021

Real trips, real photographers, never a staged shoot.

BorrowShoot real shop owners on real working days, graded warm and slightly desaturated.
Cash App

People-first stories, product just glimpsed in the action.

Borrow3–4 shop-owner mini-docs where auto-reply appears, never explained.
Workoholics WIP

Process, not polish — mid-task over posed portraits.

BorrowCandid framing: wrapping an order, typing a reply, mid-laugh — never posed.
